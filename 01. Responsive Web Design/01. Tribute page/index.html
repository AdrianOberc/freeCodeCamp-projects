Abraham Lincoln

The man who issued Emancipation Proclamation which let to abolishing slavery in the US

Here is a timeline of Abraham Lincoln's life:

  • 1809 - born in Hampshire County, (West) Virginia
  • 1816 - Lincoln's family relocates to Indiana
  • 1818 - Mother Nancy dies of milk sickness
  • 1819 - Father Thomas Lincoln Remarries Widow Sarah Bush Johnston
  • 1828 - Lincoln's Elder Sister Sarah Lincoln Dies during Childbirth
  • 1832 - Lincoln Makes First Enlistment in the Black Hawk War
  • 1834 - Lincoln is Elected to the Illinois State Legislature
  • 1837 - Lincoln is admitted to the Bar
  • 1849 - Lincoln Receives a Patent
  • 1860 - Abraham Lincoln Elected 16th President of the United States
  • 1862 - Lincoln Signs The Emancipation Act
  • 1862 - Lincoln Establishes the Department of Agriculture
  • 1865 - President Abraham Lincoln Assassinated
